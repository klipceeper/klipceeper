# Privacy

KlipCeeper Lite gives you full control and ownership over your data.

- All data is saved locally on your device
  - KlipCeeper Lite uses IndexedDB for content storage and IndexedDB is central to the working of the workbench (a feature of KlipCeeper Lite)
- KlipCeeper Lite does not connect to any external service over the network
  - KlipCeeper Lite will possibly in the future provide user-invoked ability to push content to local tools like Obsidian
- All data can be exported by the user as either Markdown or JSON
- KlipCeeper Lite does not collect any telemetry data
